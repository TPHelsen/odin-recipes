Best Lasagna recipe

Description

Making lasagna can be time consuming and might take a lot of effort, but the results are definately worth it. The key to a good lasagna is the layering and the bechamel sauce. There is no secret to this, but trial and error is the only way to find the perfect result.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Yellow onion
  • Garlic
  • Tomatoe paste
  • Crushed tomatoes
  • Red wine
  • Milk
  • Butter
  • Flour
  • Salt & Pepper
  • Lemon zest
  • Parmesan

Step by step

  1. Preheat the oven at 200 celcius
  2. Cook the meat and add tomatoe paste, crushed tomatoes, red wine, garlic and spices.
  3. Melt the butter and let it cool
  4. Mix the milk, butter and flour in a bowl
  5. Start the layering in an oven form, by first putting the meat sauce, then bechamel followed by lasagne plates. Make 3-4 layers.
