Carbonara the real way

Description

Making spaghetti carbonara the right way is the only way to make it. Even though there are many versions, the original way is one of the easiest. With only a few ingredients and a bit of patience, the carbonara will come together beautifully in the end, just like in those videos you see online.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Egg yolks
  • Guanciale
  • Parmesan
  • Black pepper
