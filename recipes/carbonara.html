Carbonara the real way

Description

Making spaghetti carbonara the right way is the only way to make it. Even though there are many versions, the original way is one of the easiest. With only a few ingredients and a bit of patience, the carbonara will come together beautifully in the end, just like in those videos you see online.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Egg yolks
  • Guanciale
  • Parmesan
  • Black pepper

Step by step

  1. Boil the pasta
  2. Cook the guanciale
  3. Mix egg yolks with parmesan, add black pepper and some pasta water until consistency is good
  4. Dry the guanciale when it is done cooking
  5. Add the guanciale to the pasta after water is rinsed
  6. Take of the heat and add the egg / parmesan mix and mix together
  7. Top with some black pepper and more parmesan.
